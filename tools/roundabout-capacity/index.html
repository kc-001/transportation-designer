Model Formula
Entry Capacity (TRL Linear)
Qe = k × F × (1 − fc × Qc)
k = geometric factor from entry angle and radius
F = 303 × v (flow scale factor)
fc = 0.21 × e−0.023D (circulating flow factor)
Degree of Saturation
x = Qc / Qe
x < 0.85 → LOS A–C · x > 1.0 → Failure
Capacity Status
