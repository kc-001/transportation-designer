TRL Formula
Capacity — TRL / Wardrop Method
Qw = 280 × w × (1 + e/w) × (1 − p/3) / (1 + w/l)
w = Carriageway width (m) · e = Average entry width (m)
l = Length of weaving section (m) · p = Proportion of weaving traffic
Valid Range
e/w: 0.4–1.0 · l: 18–90m · p: 0.2–0.8
Results outside these ranges should be treated as approximate
