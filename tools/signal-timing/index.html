Webster Formula
Optimal Cycle Length
Co = (1.5L + 5) / (1 − Y)
L = Total lost time (sec) · Y = Σ(v/s) for all critical phases
Phase Green Time
gi = (Co − L) × yi / Y
yi = flow ratio for phase i · Y = sum of all phase ratios
Typical Ranges
Cycle: 60–120 sec · Y: 0.6–0.85 · Min green: 7 sec
