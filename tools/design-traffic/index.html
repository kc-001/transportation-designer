Formulas
Cumulative Traffic
N = 365 × T₀ × TF × DF × GF
T₀ = Initial daily trucks · TF = Truck factor · DF = Lane distribution factor
Growth Factor
GF = [(1+r)ⁿ − 1] / r
r = Annual growth rate (decimal) · n = Design life (years)
msa
msa = N / 1,000,000
